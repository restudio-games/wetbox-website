About Wetbox

Wetbox Engine is a professional game development framework designed for developers who demand full transparency. There is no built‑in library — you assemble your tech stack exactly as your project demands.

Restudio Games provides starter templates and curated documentation, while the engine itself stays lean, giving you direct access to hardware through industry-standard APIs.
